Inkarelis

About Us

“Inkarelis” is one of the longest-running dance studios in Klaipėda, having started its activities more than 50 years ago. Over the decades, the studio has grown proud of its achievements and the talents it has nurtured.

Activities and Classes

The studio offers suitable classes for all ages and skill levels. Currently, more than 200 students regularly attend group lessons led by a team of professional and experienced instructors.

Mission

Our mission is to nurture talent and creativity by providing high-quality dance lessons in an engaging and friendly environment.

Additional Activities

In addition to regular classes, we also organize:

  • Events with students’ parents
  • Trips
  • Summer leisure activities

All of these are aimed at not only helping each student grow but also providing joyful and memorable experiences along the way.

"Inkarelis" dance studio – a dance studio in the southern part of Klaipėda – offers various dance style classes for students.
