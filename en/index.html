About Us

“Inkarelis” is a dance studio located in the southern part of Klaipėda. The studio takes part in the World Lithuanian and Western Region Song Festivals and is represented in national and international festivals and competitions.

“Inkarelis” offers a wide variety of dance styles – there's something for everyone, regardless of age or experience level.
