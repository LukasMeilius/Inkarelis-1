Matas Stirbys

Pop, Show Dance, Folk Dance, “Dance with Mom” Latin Group

Qualified Dance Instructor:

In 2014, Matas graduated from the Lithuanian Academy of Music and Theatre, Klaipėda Faculty with a Bachelor’s degree in Performing Arts and a teaching qualification.

Matas began his dance journey during his school years. Over time, he gained extensive experience and now actively shares his knowledge with young dancers.

When asked about his teaching goals, Matas says that every educator wants their students to achieve high results — but for him, the most important thing is to instill humanity and respect for others.

Matas’s dedication to teaching, positive outlook, and ability to foster a friendly atmosphere make him a beloved teacher both among his students and in the wider community.

In his classes, Matas develops not only technical skills but also encourages:

• communication,
• mutual respect,
• the desire to grow.

Matas eagerly awaits every lesson where he can share his passion for dance and inspire the younger generation to pursue their dreams.

Choreography studio "Inkarelis" – located in the southern part of Klaipėda – offers students a variety of dance style classes.
