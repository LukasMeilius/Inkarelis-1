Donata Meiliūtė - Paulauskienė

Pop, Show Dance, Folk Dance

Certified dance and theatre educator with a senior teacher qualification. Currently experienced not only as a dance teacher but also as the head of the studio. A positive and determined personality, always radiating good energy. Donata began dancing back in her school years and has now been working with various dance styles for over ten years.

Donata strives for her students not only to learn how to dance but to fall in love with it just as she did.

In teaching dance, she emphasizes that while good results are important, it’s even more important to nurture kindness and respect in young individuals. She believes that dance is not just a sport or art form but also a powerful tool for personal development.

Donata aims to create an open and supportive environment where students feel welcomed and safe. For her, dance classes are not just sessions — they are opportunities to build friendships and foster cooperation.

Choreography studio "Inkarelis" – located in the southern part of Klaipėda – offers students a variety of dance style classes.
